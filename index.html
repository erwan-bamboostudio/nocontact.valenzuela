OUR DEDICATION TO KEEPING VALENZUELA CITY SAFE

This is the official website of the No Contact Apprehension Traffic Program for the Province of Bataan. If you are here to verify a notice of violation or to check your plate number, click below.

OUR MISSION

With this growing population and multiple road infrastructure projects, it is our mission to implement a system that will help make our roads safer for our fellow motorists and citizens alike. With a No Contact Apprehension Program, our province can effectively combat the safety risks related to ongoing traffic congestion, reckless driving and the spread of the coronavirus through traffic stops.
